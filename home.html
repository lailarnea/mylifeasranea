My Life Experiences

Middle School

I went to SK Bandar Baru Bangi and Tahfiz Abim Damai to get my Islamic Academic after school from 2018 until 2013. It was very hard for to attend 2 schools at very young age, but I am very grateful to my both parents to signed me up to this Tahfiz,I met more friends from different classes in middle school. Two schools means everything is doubled, double friends, double books, and double examinations of course. I was a very smart student during back then. I managed to get 4As for my UPSR and Mumtaz grade for my SPRA.

High School

For high school, I went to SMK Bandar Baru Bangi. I was a Computer Science student where only 5 girls in the class and all the rests are boys.I graduated high school with flying colours, thanks to my mom for always nagging to study hard.I got 6As for my SPM and worked at her office during part times.

University

Thanks to my SPM results, I got accepted to UiTM. At first, I was very sad after knowing that I will further my studies at Perlis.Since it is very far from my home, I was very worried and scared. After week adapting,I realised I actually liked Perlis more than I expected.I met many friends from different backgrounds. It opened my eyes that is more many to life for. A bit sad that we are now in pandemic and have to attend online classed instead, I do miss having physical classes.
